Allergy Treatment

Comprehensive diagnostic patch testing and management for skin allergies, urticaria, and contact dermatitis in a clinical setting.

Clinical Allergy Management

Skin allergies can be persistent and difficult to manage without identifying the underlying trigger. Common symptoms include itching, redness, hives (Urticaria), and eczema patches. At DermaTales, we focus on evidence-based diagnostics, including Patch Testing to identity contact allergens ranging from cosmetics to metals and fragrances.

Our board-certified dermatologists provide customized management plans that include lifestyle modifications, targeted topicals, and advanced systemic therapies for long-term relief from chronic flare-ups.

When to Seek Treatment

  • Persistent, unexplained itching or redness recurring frequently.
  • Sudden onset of hives (urticaria) that lasts longer than a few days.
  • Skin reactions to cosmetics, jewelry, hair dye, or new skincare products.

Emergency Situations

  • Anaphylaxis Warning: If your rash is accompanied by swelling of lips/throat, dizziness, or difficulty breathing, visit an emergency room immediately rather than an outpatient clinic.

The Diagnostic & Treatment Process

Clinical Evaluation

A deep dive into your medical history, exposure history, and thorough skin examination by our MD dermatologists.

Advanced Patch Testing

Application of standard antigen panels on the back to safely identify specific contact allergens over a 48-72 hour period.

Trigger Identification

Reading of test results to isolate exactly which chemical, metal, or substance caused the hypersensitivity reaction.

Personalized Management Plan

Prescription medications combined with a detailed list of safe products and ingredients to avoid in the future.
