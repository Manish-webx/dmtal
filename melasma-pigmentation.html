Melasma & Pigmentation

Advanced multi-modal protocols for stubborn melasma and deep pigmentation. Achieve an even, luminous complexion with expert-led care.

Deep Pigment Correction

Melasma and stubborn pigmentation are complex conditions often triggered by hormonal shifts, sun exposure, and genetic factors. These conditions require a stabilized approach that goes beyond surface brightening. At DermaTales, we prioritize evidence-based protocols that target the melanin-producing cells (Melanocytes) while preserving skin barrier health.

Our dermatologists customize every treatment plan using a combination of Q-Switched lasers, medical-grade chemical peels, and targeted actives to deliver a visible, lasting glow.

How Pigment Correction Works

Our multi-modal approach focuses on **Stabilization and Resurfacing**. We use Laser Toning to gently break down deep-seated pigment without heat-induced irritation (which can worsen melasma). This is coupled with clinical chemical reconstruction to exfoliate surface irregularities and block the enzymes responsible for pigment production, resulting in a gradual and safe brightening of the skin.

The Benefits
