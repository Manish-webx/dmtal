Cyst & Lipoma Treatment

Minimally invasive surgical excision of sebaceous cysts and lipomas. Precision removal with hidden incisions and rapid recovery.

Precision Lump Removal

Cysts and lipomas are common benign growths under the skin that can become painful, infected, or cosmetically distracting. Sebaceous cysts are fluid-filled sacs, while lipomas are slow-growing fatty lumps. At DermaTales, we specialize in "minimal-incision" surgery that allows for complete removal while prioritizing the aesthetic outcome.

Our dermatologists use internal suturing and clinical-grade closure techniques to ensure that any visible marks are minimized, giving you a smooth, lump-free skin surface.

How Treatment Works

The excision is a sterile surgical procedure performed under local anesthesia. We make a tiny, strategically placed incision to extract the cyst wall or the lipoma mass entirely. Removing the entire capsule is critical to preventing recurrence. The wound is then closed with precision sutures that are often hidden within the skin layers.

The Benefits

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals with visible, growing lumps under the skin.
  • Those experiencing tenderness or pain from a cyst pressing on nerves.
  • Patients seeking removal for purely cosmetic reasons.

Infectious Care

  • Active Infection: If a cyst is currently infected and inflamed, we will prescribe antibiotics and drain it first before full surgical excision can take place.
