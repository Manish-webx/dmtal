Psoriasis & Vitiligo

Specialized clinical management for Psoriasis and Vitiligo using targeted Phototherapy, Biologicals, and advanced grafting techniques.

Specialized Immunity Care

Psoriasis and Vitiligo are chronic conditions rooted in the immune system. While Psoriasis involves rapid skin cell buildup, Vitiligo results from a loss of pigment (melanin). At DermaTales, we utilize state-of-the-art **Narrowband UVB (NBUVB)** therapy and **Biologics** to manage flare-ups and promote repigmentation.

For stable Vitiligo patches, we also offer **Melanocyte Transfer** and **Skin Grafting** procedures to permanently restore your skin's natural color with high success rates.
