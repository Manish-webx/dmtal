Facial Pigmentation

Targeted correction for sunspots, age spots, and uneven facial tone. Restore your skin's natural even tone with laser-grade precision.

Restore Your Facial Clarity

Facial pigmentation, often manifesting as freckles, sunspots (lentigines), or age spots, occurs when localized areas of skin produce excessive melanin. These spots are typically caused by prolonged UV exposure or environmental pollution. At DermaTales, we use precision-targeted laser technology to break down these clusters of pigment without affecting the surrounding healthy skin.

Our goal is to achieve a balanced, radiant complexion that reflects light evenly, giving you a naturally refreshed and youthful appearance.

How Correction Works

We utilize the **Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser**, which delivers high-intensity light in ultra-short pulses. This energy is absorbed by the pigmented lesions, causing them to shatter into microscopic particles. Your body's lymphatic system then naturally clears these particles over time. For surface pigmentation, we also offer medical-grade resurfacing that clarifies the skin surface instantly.
