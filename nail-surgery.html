Nail Surgery

Radical relief for painful ingrown nails and matrix disorders. Professional clinical care with a focus on permanent solutions.

Clinical Nail Excellence

Ingrown toenails (Onychocryptosis) cause severe pain, swelling, and recurrent infections that can limit your mobility and comfort. At DermaTales, we go beyond temporary clipping to provide permanent surgical relief. Using advanced phenolisation and matrix repair, we ensure that the problematic nail edge never grows back.

Our approach is minimally invasive, focusing on preserving the natural aesthetic of the nail while providing radical relief from chronic pain and infection.

How Nail Surgery Works

The procedure is a definitive cure for ingrown nails. We perform a **Partial Nail Avulsion** under a digital block (local anesthesia) to remove the small sliver of nail that is digging into the skin. To prevent recurrence, we apply **Phenol** to the nail matrix (the root), which permanently stops that specific edge from growing, with a 98% success rate.

The Benefits

Permanent Relief

98% success rate in preventing recurrent ingrown nails.

Walking Readiness

Most patients can walk comfortably immediately after the procedure.

Matrix Repair

Corrects the root cause rather than just treating symptoms.

The DermaTales Procedure Steps

Clinical Assessment

Examination of the nail fold and identification of infection levels.

Digital Block

Local anesthesia at the base of the toe for a totally painless procedure.

Phenolisation

Chemical destruction of the specific nail root edge to prevent regrowth.

Surgical Dressing

Application of medicated pressure dressing for optimal healing.
