How Acne Scar Treatment Works

Acne scar treatment is a process of **Dermal Remodeling**. Whether using lasers or microneedling, the objective is to stimulate the body's natural healing response. By creating controlled micro-injuries in the scarred tissue, we trigger the production of new, healthy collagen and elastin. Over several sessions, this "fills in" the depressions of pitted scars and smoothens the surface irregularities, leading to a visible restoration of skin texture.

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals with healed acne but persistent scarring.
  • Those with realistic expectations of skin improvement.
  • Patients committed to a series of sessions for best results.
  • Non-smokers or those willing to pause during treatment.

Who Should Avoid

  • Individuals with active, inflammatory acne.
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women.
  • Those with a history of keloidal tendencies.
  • Patients with active skin infections near the area.
