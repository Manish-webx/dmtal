Psoriasis & Vitiligo

Specialized clinical management for Psoriasis and Vitiligo using targeted Phototherapy, Biologics, and advanced grafting techniques.

Specialized Immunity Care

Psoriasis and Vitiligo are chronic conditions rooted in the immune system. While Psoriasis involves rapid skin cell buildup causing scaly plaques, Vitiligo results from a loss of pigment (melanin) leading to white patches. At DermaTales, we utilize state-of-the-art Narrowband UVB (NBUVB) therapy and Biologics to manage flare-ups and promote repigmentation.

For stable Vitiligo patches, we also offer Melanocyte Transfer and Skin Grafting procedures to permanently restore your skin's natural color with high success rates. Our approach is deeply personalized, addressing both the medical and emotional impact of these conditions.

How Psoriasis & Vitiligo Treatment Works

Treatment for both conditions focuses on modulating the immune response. For Psoriasis, Narrowband UVB phototherapy slows down the rapid cell cycle, reducing plaque formation while Biologics target specific immune proteins. For Vitiligo, NBUVB stimulates dormant melanocytes to repigment the patches. In stable, non-spreading cases, surgical options like Melanocyte Transfer and Split Thickness Skin Grafting provide a more definitive and permanent restoration of natural skin color.

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals diagnosed with plaque, guttate, or scalp Psoriasis.
  • Those with stable, non-spreading Vitiligo patches seeking repigmentation.
  • Patients seeking long-term management over short-term topical relief.
  • Anyone who has not responded adequately to topical treatments alone.

Who Should Avoid

  • Patients with actively spreading (unstable) Vitiligo for surgical options.
  • Individuals with a history of photosensitivity disorders.
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women (for certain Biologic treatments).
  • Those with active systemic infections (for Biologic therapy).
