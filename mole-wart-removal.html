Mole & Wart Removal

Advanced skin lesion removal using Radiofrequency and Laser technology. Safe, effective, and designed for minimal scarring.

Precision Skin Clearing

Skin lesions like moles and warts can be sources of physical irritation and aesthetic concern. At DermaTales, we use minimally invasive techniques to precisely target and remove these growths from the root, ensuring healthy surrounding skin remains untouched.

Our dermatologists use high-frequency RF ablation and CO2 lasers to provide a permanent solution for warts and cosmetically concerning moles with superior healing outcomes.

How Mole & Wart Removal Works

The procedure involves using targeted energy (Radiofrequency or Laser) to vaporize the lesion layer by layer. This results in instant removal with immediate coagulation, leading to a bloodless procedure and significantly faster healing than traditional surgical excision. By precisely controlling the depth of removal, we minimize the risk of scarring.

Ideal Candidates

  • Individuals with non-cancerous skin moles.
  • Those with persistent viral warts or skin tags.
  • Anyone seeking cosmetic removal of skin lesions.

Who Should Avoid

  • Moles showing suspicious signs of malignancy.
  • Patients with uncontrolled keloidal tendencies.
  • Active infections at the site of removal.
